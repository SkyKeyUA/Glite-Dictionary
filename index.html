Morphology

jewelry = jewel + ry (transparent)
The noun is a straightforward collective/relational formation from 'jewel' meaning items made of or consisting of jewels.

Etymology

jewelry comes from jewel, which once came from an old word meaning 'plaything' or 'delight'. So jewelry means small, decorative things you wear to look and feel special.
